WHO ARE WE

OVERVIEW

Founded in 1949, Imrooz is one of Pakistan’s oldest business houses, with a diverse portfolio of businesses, ranging from trading of Industrial Raw Materials, Representation of Overseas Companies, Consumer Product Distribution to Music and Broadcast Monitoring and Media Intelligence.

Imrooz started as a small family owned business in 1949 from the Eastern part of the newly created Pakistan. More than six decades later, Imrooz is still a family owned business and has grown to encompass many diverse business areas.

The owning family has its own business constitution that states its beliefs and ethics and governs the way all its businesses are conducted. The Imrooz constitution has been reviewed as a case study and used in various institutions as an example of an efficient family owned operations governing document.

Imrooz’s success is due to a deep belief in the welfare of its people, work ethics and business commitment. These beliefs form the basis of everything it does.

VISION

We aim to create value partnerships and serve the society we exist in through an ethical and sustainable family business model

MISSION

Imrooz family continues to evolve with emerging market opportunities to enhance value of businesses for its customers, suppliers, stakeholders and people and create strategic alliances for long-term profitability.

VALUES

  • Compassion (society, staff, business partners)

  • Integrity

  • Collaborative services

  • Respect and humility
