OUR FOUNDER

ASHARAF RIAZ

Mr. Ashraf Riaz was born in Lyallpur, India in 1920. A graduate from the prestigious Government College Lahore, he started his professional career as a clerk in a government office in Calcutta. Later he joined the famous tea company Isphani and Co. Post the partition of India in 1947, he moved to Karachi and remained with the Isphani group, managing their airline business Orient Airways (which later became Pakistan International Airlines).

Mr. Riaz always had an entrepreneurial spirit and wanted to have his own business. Encouraged by his colleagues and family, in 1949 he laid the foundation for Imrooz and set up a small office in Karachi, in what was then East Pakistan (now Bangladesh), selling bicycle spare parts. Over the years, under his leadership, Imrooz grew from its humble beginnings to expand into industrial raw material agency, representation and trading, along with milk-food manufacturing in the early ‘70s.

Though Mr.Riaz’s untimely death in 1974 was a big setback for the family, he left behind a strong legacy and values that his sons carried forward for future generations. Today, the Ashraf Riaz family business is recognized as a well-established and successful business endeavour led with integrity and high values.

Now entering its third generation, Mr. Ashraf Riaz’s grandchildren are being inducted into the business under a very carefully managed programme supervised by his three sons and current directors, Mr. Naveed Riaz, Mr. Ateed Riaz and Mr. Ameed Riaz, to carry on the family legacy.

Imrooz acquire the Pakistan interest of G. Atherton & Co., a 100 year old Liverpool based company, which had offices in India and Pakistan. To merge both houses, a new entity Atherton & Imrooz is formed. This company then goes on to specialize in representing quality foreign Industrial Raw Material Manufacturers.
