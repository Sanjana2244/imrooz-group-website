First Imrooz
Modaraba

First Imrooz Modaraba (FIM) is a perpetual Modaraba, initially floated for the specific purpose of trading in approved commodities and other products and was converted to a multipurpose Modaraba on August 8, 1998. FIM does not provide any financial services that are normally associated with the Modaraba sector. It specializes in the import, warehousing and distribution of Industrial Raw Materials to a number of industries in Pakistan. Industries serviced by FIM include Coatings (Paints and Inks), Construction, Personal Care (Shampoos, Soaps, Toothpastes, Creams etc.) as well as Textiles, Food, Pharmaceuticals, Agro Pesticides and Fishing.
