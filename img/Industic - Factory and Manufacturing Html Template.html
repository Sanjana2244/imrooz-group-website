ABOUT US

A Leading Industry of Raw Materials, Serving Since 1994

First ImroozModaraba (FIM) has been operational since 1994 and is listed on the Karachi Stock Exchange. The Modaraba primarily trades in industrial raw materials, catering to numerous industries like paint, coatings, pharmaceuticals, food, agriculture, personal care, household,textile etc. The common perception of a Modaraba is that it is a ﬁnancial institution, however FIM does not offer any ﬁnancial services.

First ImroozModaraba (FIM) is managed by AR Management Services (Pvt) Limited, having its head oﬃce in Karachi with a branch oﬃce in Lahore.

CONSTRUCTIONS & COATINGS

  • Raw materials for Paint industries like pigments, biocides, anti-settling, anti-foaming and anti-skinning agents.

  • Raw material for master batches.

  • Raw material for grease manufacturing.

Food & Pharmaceuticals

  • Food colors for food industries.

  • Food ﬂavors for ice creams, biscuits and confectionery, drinks and savory industries.

  • Phosphates for baking industries.

  • Surfactants for pharmaceutical industries.

  • Pharmaceutical ingredients for tablets coating, thickness and foaming agents for toothpaste, etc.

Personal Care, Cosmetics & Household

  • Surfactants for personal care industries.

  • Surfactants for detergent industries.

  • Pigments for soaps.

Textile, Agro & Leather

  • Raw materials for textile industries to be used in printing, dyeing and processing.

  • Raw materials for adhesive and glues.

  • Surfactants for pesticides.

  • Biocides, tanning agents and surfactants for leather.
